About Us

We are a non-profit organization dedicated to providing food to underprivileged communities across India. Our mission is to reduce food waste and alleviate hunger by connecting donors with NGOs and food trusts.

Our Services

We offer the following services:

  • Food collection from restaurants, weddings, and events
  • Food distribution to NGOs and food trusts
  • Volunteer opportunities for individuals and organizations
  • Education and awareness programs on food waste and hunger

Marriage Waste Food Donors

We work with individuals and organizations who donate excess food from weddings and events to help reduce food waste. If you have leftover food from your marriage or event, consider donating it to us.
